Program Overview
Google Cloud APAC FinOps & Procurement Program · April 2026

Program Description

A data-led, hybrid engagement initiative designed to position GCP Marketplace as the strategic procurement and cost-optimisation layer for enterprises across India, Southeast Asia, and ANZ. The program runs across 5 execution stages over 12–14 weeks, targeting Finance and Procurement leaders (CFOs, CPOs, FinOps practitioners) across 228 mapped accounts and 4,388 contacts.
